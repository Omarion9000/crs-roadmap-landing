# PROJECT_CONTEXT.md

## Project Name
CRS Roadmap

## Project Overview
CRS Roadmap is a SaaS platform designed for international students and temporary residents in Canada who want to improve their CRS (Comprehensive Ranking System) score for Express Entry.

The platform allows users to simulate strategies to increase their CRS score and generate a prioritized roadmap based on the ROI of each improvement.

Target users:
- International students in Canada
- PGWP holders
- Skilled workers planning Express Entry

Core idea:
Instead of guessing how to improve their CRS score, users receive a ranked strategy roadmap.

Example:
1. Improve English CLB 7 → CLB 9 (+34 CRS)
2. Learn French CLB 7 (+25 CRS)
3. Gain Canadian work experience (+40 CRS)
